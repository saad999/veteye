FEATURES

Customization

In the setting page you can customize the app according to your field, by selecting one or more than one category. Select the order in which you want to display the whole drug list. Also you could schedule the notifications or cancel it. By using the provided features you may achieve a great benefit.

Exploring

In the index section you could explore the available drug date base, by showing the available generic names, actions and companies. Just click to copy and paste it on the search box.
